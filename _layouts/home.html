Where to stay

We suggest finding a place on Airbnb, especially near our house in the Mission, or in the Richmond District (in San Francisco, not the East Bay city). Lombard St has modestly priced motels and is close to the Presidio. SFO airport also has many reasonably priced chain hotels nearby.

We love showing people a good time in the city so get in touch with us if you have any questions about where to stay!
