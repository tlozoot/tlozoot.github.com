About the couple

Jon and Kitt met as Tech Company Interns in 2011. After a whirlwind trip through New Orleans, Costa Rica, and Zion National Park, they finally decided to settle down together in San Francisco. There they bonded over (or learned to tolerate) hiking, guitar solos, and Bachelor in Paradise.

Where to stay

We suggest finding a place on Airbnb, especially in the nearby Richmond, or near our house in the Mission. Lombard St has modestly priced motels and is close to the Presidio. SFO airport also has many reasonably priced chain hotels nearby.

Most hotels are close to downtown SF, but they tend to be expensive and/or in high-crime areas. We love showing people a good time in the city so get in touch with us if you have any questions about where to stay!
